Positive and negative aspects of the previous IVs

We found a really nice interactive data representation called Windy.com (IV1 on the list) It's a world map overlaid by wind direction and speed.
It's easy, intuitive and very clear, however it contains tons of options.
The arrows show direction and the color show speed.
Otherwise it works just like google maps. For more detailed information about an specific location:
just click on a point in the map.

We found a bad example within the page genome.jp (IV2 on the list)
They are metabolic interaction pathways depicted by lines and nodes with names. All the nodes are links to other pages.
All the lines show the connections of each node.
The main downside of this IV is that it is very cluttered and therefore is really time consuming to try to look up something specificly. This IV could be improved by
adding some interactivity and reducing complexity.
